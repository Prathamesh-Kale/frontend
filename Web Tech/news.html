India celebrates 77th Independance Day

Awashed in the zeal and patriotic colours of the country,
India will celebrate 77th Independence Day on August 15, 2023.
With preparations on full throttle, institutions are in full gear to celebrate the day where students and children prep for different roles including some hard-hitting speeches.
Many schools, and colleges would be celebrating the ‘Azaadi ka Amrit Mahotsava’, with the theme 'Nation First, Always First'.

Alia

Alia Bhatt is the daughter of filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt and actress Soni Razdan. She made her acting debut with Sangharsh, as a child artist in 1999.
She played her first leading role in Karan Johar's teen film Student of the Year in 2012.
She is one of the highest-paid actors of Bollywood who has won multiple awards for her film roles.
She made a mark in the Hindi film industry with Highway, 2 States, Dear Zindagi and Gangubai Kathiawadi.
She got married to Ranbir Kapoor.
